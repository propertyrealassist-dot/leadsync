Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 30, 2024

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LeadSync collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information. By using our Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you directly provide when you:

  • Create an account: Name, email address, password, phone number, company name
  • Use the Service: AI strategy configurations, conversation data, lead information, appointment details
  • Contact support: Support tickets, feedback, and communications
  • Make payments: Billing information (processed by third-party payment processors)
  • Integrate services: API keys, OAuth tokens, third-party credentials

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use LeadSync:

  • Usage data: Features used, actions taken, time spent, click patterns
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Log data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, referral URLs
  • Cookies: Session tokens, preferences, analytics data

1.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Integrations: Data from GoHighLevel, CRMs, and other connected services
  • Authentication: OAuth profile information if you sign in with third-party accounts
  • Payment processors: Transaction confirmation data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Purpose Examples
Provide the Service Process conversations, manage leads, schedule appointments, sync integrations
AI Features Train and improve AI models, generate responses, qualify leads
Account Management Create and maintain accounts, authenticate users, manage teams
Communications Send service updates, respond to inquiries, provide support
Analytics Generate reports, track performance, provide insights

Legal Compliance Meet legal obligations, enforce Terms, resolve disputes

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 We Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS/Azure), email delivery, payment processing, analytics tools
  • Third-Party Integrations: GoHighLevel, CRMs, and services you connect
  • Team Members: Other users in your organization (based on permissions)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights

3.2 We Do NOT Share:

  • Your data with advertisers or marketers
  • Personal information for unrelated purposes
  • Conversation data with unauthorized parties

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions, multi-factor authentication
  • Infrastructure: SOC 2 compliant hosting providers
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining secure passwords and protecting your account credentials.

5. Data Retention

5.1 How Long We Keep Data:

  • Account data: While your account is active plus 90 days after deletion
  • Conversation data: Retained based on your settings or subscription plan
  • Billing records: 7 years for tax and accounting compliance
  • Logs and analytics: 12-24 months for operational purposes
  • Backups: Up to 90 days in encrypted backup storage

5.2 Data Deletion:

You can request data deletion by:

  • Deleting your account through settings
  • Contacting support at privacy@leadsync.com

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance or dispute resolution.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it

  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)

To exercise these rights, email privacy@leadsync.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.
